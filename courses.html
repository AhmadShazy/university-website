Degree Programs

🎓

BS Computer Science (BSCS)

4-year undergraduate program covering software engineering, AI, data science, systems programming, and cloud computing. HEC recognized, 130 credit hours.

Undergraduate

🎓

BS Software Engineering (BSSE)

4-year program focused on software development lifecycle, DevOps, agile methodologies, and enterprise systems. 130 credit hours.

Undergraduate

🔬

MS Computer Science (MSCS)

2-year graduate program with specializations in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Distributed Systems. Thesis and coursework options.

Graduate

🏅

PhD Computer Science

Doctoral program for original research contributions. Internationally recognized faculty supervision. Areas: AI, HCI, Networks, and Algorithms.
