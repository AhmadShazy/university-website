Eligibility Criteria

Program Minimum Qualification Minimum Marks Entry Test Duration
BSCS Intermediate (Pre-Engineering / ICS) 50% aggregate NAT-I / COMSATS Test 4 Years
BSSE Intermediate (Pre-Engineering / ICS) 50% aggregate NAT-I / COMSATS Test 4 Years
MSCS BS (CS / SE / IT) or equivalent 2.5 CGPA or 60% GAT-General / GRE 2 Years
PhD CS MS / MPhil in CS or related field 3.0 CGPA or 70% GAT-Subject / GRE 3–5 Years

Admission Process

📝

Step 1: Apply Online

Submit application via COMSATS admission portal with required documents.

📋

Step 2: Entry Test

Appear in NAT or COMSATS entry test. Merit is calculated on marks + test score.

📊

Step 3: Merit List

Merit list published on university website. Check your status online.

🎓

Step 4: Enrollment

Pay fee and submit documents at the admissions office to confirm enrollment.
